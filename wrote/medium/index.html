Medium

I’ve been aware of Medium — Twitter’s platform for sharing long-form stories — for some time. But today, I feel like I really unearthed the potential of the thing.

Initially, I’d kind of shrugged Medium off. I didn’t want to give it much of my attention on the assumption that the world had enough hosted, template driven writing platforms. “What? A bigger, more exclusive, better funded version of Svbtle? I’ve seen this show before.” That sort of thing.

But today, I read an article about a great distance collaboration tool. Then another about the desirability of human-readable email error messages. Then another about a potential marketplace for sought after Twitter usernames. I just kept opening new articles, and they just kept exceeding my expectations. (Sidebar: the section called ‘Products I Wish Existed’ is fantastic.)

I’m not quite sure what struck me about Medium this go ‘round. I think it’s partly the beauty and simplicity of the design. But it’s also the writing… which is so good: clear, crisp and accessible. I don’t read writing like that very often — at least not on the web — and it was refreshing.

Good stuff.

The New "Read & Trust Magazine"

About a year and a half ago, I had the honor of being one of the founding members of The Read & Trust Network. Read & Trust was founded with a simple goal: gathering together the best independent writers available — the ones recommended by the writers you read and trust.

The concept was seemingly simple: for a low price of $5 per month, you could subscribe to a newsletter where you’d receive exclusive content from incredible writers, each and every week. And wouldn’t you know, it worked. People loved it.

So a year and a half later — with the concept proven and lessons learned — R&T’s founder Aaron Mahnke has decided to take it to another level. The weekly email newsletter is being upgraded to amonthly digital magazine with more content, better design and wider availability.

As Aaron describes it:

Rather than the weekly emails that you’ve been used to, the Read & Trust Magazine will now be a monthly collection of articles from five contributing members. The text is still the star, but the content will be delivered in a way that is better for everyone.

So if reading exclusive, insightful content from some of the best independent writers on the web is something that appeals to you — go ahead and check it out. And if you’re not sure you’re ready to make the jump and become a subscriber, consider buying a single edition for just $6. I can’t recommend it more emphatically.

Dream big dreams

You are awesome, and you can do amazing things. Those other people — the ones creating the things you love and admire — are just like you. There’s nothing magical about them.

The things that separate “them” from you are — happily enough — things over which you have total control: imagination and focus.

Most people don’t bother with either of these things. Lacking imagination, they let themselves get slotted into whatever role someone else prescribes for them. They walk the trodden path. Or lacking focus, they squander their time and attention to the point that whatever goals they had become unattainable. Or both.

But you have control over your own actions. Dream big dreams. When you have an idea, let it take root. Imagine a world where that idea became reality. Then, work backwards.

What will it take to make that happen? You probably don’t know, and that’s okay. Often big dreams are so far removed from the present that its hard to get your head around all the steps you’ll need to take. So instead, focus on the next thing you can do to move the proverbial ball forward. Then do that. Refocus. Repeat. Repeat. Repeat.

Says hello

Hello! My name is Ian Patrick Hines. I’m a political consultant, web designer, and community organizing strategist based in Severna Park, Maryland. I’m the founder and president of The BEAG Co., where I help political campaigns and nonprofit organizations build & leverage the digital infrastructure they need to succeed. Before that, I was an organizer at NationBuilder—helping create the world’s first community organizing system.

When I was growing up, my father taught me two lessons that really stuck: (1) know a little bit about a lot of things, and a lot about a few, and (2) the key to success is consistently executing on the fundamentals. I try to bring that wisdom to work with me every day, and it’s served me well.

Speaking of growing up, I graduated from high school about three miles from where I live now—in Anne Arundel County—where my family has lived for five generations. Where I’m from is a central part of who I am, and I can’t make it through a conversation without talking about how much I love my home town.

Besides my work, I’m passionate about my wife, my three children, and Maryland blue crabs steamed in Old Bay. For fun, I collect baseball hats, t-shirts, and tattoos.

You can connect with me on Facebook or Twitter, or you can send me an email.

Colophon

I’ve had my own website since 2003, and in 2009 I wiped the slate clean and began writing on an eponymous domain name. It’s gone through various iterations—ianhines.com, ianhin.es, ianpatrickhines.com, and ianpatrickhin.es—but it’s always been me.

The current version was built using the following tools: Codekit, Siteleaf, & Sublime Text. It’s hosted on GitHub Pages. You can see it’s public repository, here.

Typographically, we’re using Whitney for headlines and accents and Sentinel for body copy—both of which are being served by Hoefler & Co.’s cloud.typography.com.
